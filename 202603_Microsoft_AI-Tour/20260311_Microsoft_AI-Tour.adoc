== 09:00 AM - 09:45 AM : Build AI factories for agentic and physical intelligence with NVIDIA and Microsoft Azure

* URL du talk : https://aitour.microsoft.com/flow/microsoft/paris26/sessioncatalog/page/sessioncatalog/session/1762868726911001Rk7T
* Speaker : Nicolas Savides, Microsoft Alliance, Manager EMEA, NVIDIA

.Abstract
----
Organizations looking to transform enterprise operations and manufacturing with AI need a full-stack platform that delivers intelligence at scale. NVIDIA and Microsoft Azure enable the next generation of AI factories—integrating agentic and physical intelligence to unlock new capabilities, accelerate innovation, and drive operational efficiency.
